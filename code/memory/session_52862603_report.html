Report.

General Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compact SUV designed with a modern and robust exterior. It offers both petrol and diesel engine options, providing a blend of performance and efficiency. The vehicle comfortably seats five passengers and has a boot capacity of 364 litres.

  • Design and Styling: Dynamic and robust exterior design.
  • Engine and Performance: Petrol and diesel engine options.
  • Dimensions and Capacity: Length of 3990 mm, a width of 1821 mm.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Petrol variants range from 17.96 kmpl to 20.1 kmpl.

Luxury Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is designed to provide a luxurious and comfortable experience with premium materials and advanced technology.

  • Interior Design: Soft-touch leatherette, premium seats.
  • Infotainment System: High-definition touchscreen, Amazon Alexa, Harman Kardon audio.
  • Comfort and Convenience: Panoramic sunroof, cooled glovebox, keyless entry.
  • Advanced Technology: Smart steering modes, automatic wipers, digital instrument cluster.

Safety Features.

Mahindra has prioritized safety in the XUV 3XO, equipping it with active and passive safety features, including ADAS and a robust build, achieving a 5-star Bharat NCAP rating.

  • Active Safety Features: ABS with EBD and BA.
  • Passive Safety Features: Up to 6 airbags, robust build.
  • Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS): Level 2 ADAS.

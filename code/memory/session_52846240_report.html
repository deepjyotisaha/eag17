Engine Specifications

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is available with multiple engine options:

  • 1.2L Turbo Petrol: Available with 6-speed MT Manual and 6-speed TC Automatic transmission, producing 111PS of power at 5000rpm and 200Nm of torque between 1500rpm and 3750rpm.
  • 1.2L Turbo Petrol (131PS): Comes with a 6-speed MT Manual and a 6-speed TC Automatic transmission, delivering 131PS of power at 5000rpm and 230Nm of torque between 1500rpm and 3750rpm.
  • 1.5L Turbo Diesel: Equipped with a 6-speed MT Manual and a 6-speed AMT Automatic transmission, rated to deliver 117PS of power at 3750rpm and 300Nm of torque between 1500rpm and 2500rpm.

Safety Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO has achieved a 5-star safety rating in Bharat NCAP crash tests, demonstrating high levels of safety for both adult and child occupants [1]. Key safety features include:

  • Airbags: Equipped with six airbags as standard across all trims.
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC): Enhances vehicle stability and control.
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS): Improves braking efficiency.
  • Seat Belt Reminders: Available for all seating positions.
  • Level 2 ADAS: Higher-end variants offer features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and autonomous emergency braking.

Infotainment System

The Mahindra XUV 3XO features a smart infotainment system with:

  • Built-in Amazon Alexa for entertainment and control.
  • Harman Kardon 7-speaker audio system.
  • Wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay connectivity.
  • ADRENOX Connect technology for remote control of car features via smartphone.

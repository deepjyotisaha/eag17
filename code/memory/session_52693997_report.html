This report provides a detailed overview of the Mahindra XUV 3XO, covering its key features and specifications. The XUV 3XO is a compact SUV offering a range of engine options, advanced safety features, modern technology, and a stylish design.

Engine Options

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers multiple engine options to cater to different performance needs:

  • 1.2-litre Turbo MPFI Petrol: Producing 110 PS.
  • 1.2-litre TGDi Turbo Petrol: Delivering 130 PS and 230 Nm of torque.
  • 1.5-litre Diesel: Outputting 117 PS.

These engines are available with manual, AMT, and 6-speed torque converter options. The 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine is the most powerful in its class, making 130PS/230Nm.

Safety Features

The XUV 3XO is equipped with a comprehensive suite of safety features, including:

  • Level 2 ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) with features like forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control.
  • 6 Airbags
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD)
  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP)
  • Hill Start Assist

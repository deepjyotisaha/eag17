🎯 Executive Summary

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compact SUV offering a blend of performance, safety, and technology. Key features include powerful engine options, advanced safety features like Level 2 ADAS, and a spacious interior with modern amenities. The vehicle is available in both petrol and diesel variants with manual and automatic transmission options. Its dimensions provide a balanced combination of urban maneuverability and interior space.

🔍 Market Landscape Overview

The Mahindra XUV 3XO competes in the compact SUV segment, rivaling models like Hyundai Creta, Tata Nexon, and Maruti Suzuki Brezza. It aims to offer a compelling package with its strong engine performance, advanced features, and competitive pricing.

🛠️ Detailed Features Breakdown

Standout Features:

  • Soft touch material on dashboard and door pads.
  • Panoramic sunroof - largest in the segment.
  • Level 2 ADAS with features such as forward collision warning, adaptive cruise control.
  • 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine makes 130PS/230Nm – most powerful in its class.

⚙️ Engine Specifications

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers multiple engine options:

  • Petrol Engine: 1197 cc, 128.73bhp@5000rpm, 230Nm@1500-3750rpm
  • Diesel Engine: 1498 cc, 115.05 kW @3750rpm, 300Nm@1500-2500rpm

It is available with Manual & Automatic transmission. Fuel type includes both Petrol and Diesel.

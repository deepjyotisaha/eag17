🎯 Executive Summary

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a well-rounded sub-compact SUV offering a blend of performance, luxury, and safety. Key selling points include its 5-star safety rating, Level 2 ADAS, panoramic sunroof, and Harman Kardon audio system. Potential drawbacks include limited boot space and add-on accessory costs.

🚗 General Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO comes with a variety of engine options, including both petrol and diesel variants. The petrol engines include a 1.2-liter mStallion TCMPFi and a TGDi turbo-petrol engine. The diesel engine is a 1.5-liter unit. These engines are available with both manual and automatic transmissions, catering to different driving preferences.

Engine Options:

  • 1.2-liter mStallion TCMPFi petrol (109 bhp, 200 Nm)
  • 1.2-liter TGDi turbo-petrol (128.73 bhp, 230 Nm)
  • 1.5-liter diesel (115.05 kW, 300 Nm)

Transmission:

  • 6-speed Manual
  • 6-speed Automatic (TC or AMT, depending on engine variant)

Dimensions:

  • Length: 3990 mm
  • Width: 1821 mm
  • Height: 1647 mm
  • Wheelbase: 2600 mm
  • Boot Space: 364 liters

Mileage:

  • Petrol: 17.96 - 20.10 kmpl
  • Diesel: 20.6 kmpl

The XUV 3XO's dimensions position it well within the sub-compact SUV category, offering a decent amount of space for passengers while maintaining a compact footprint suitable for urban environments. The boot space, however, may be a limiting factor for some buyers.

🛡️ Safety Features

Safety is a key selling point for the XUV 3XO, with Mahindra emphasizing both active and passive safety systems.

Active Safety Systems:

  • ABS with EBD: Prevents wheel lock-up during braking, enhancing control.
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC): Helps maintain vehicle stability during sudden maneuvers.
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS): Alerts the driver to changes in tire pressure.

Passive Safety Systems:

  • Multiple Airbags: Including driver, passenger, side, and curtain airbags, providing comprehensive occupant protection.
  • ISOFIX Child Seat Anchors: Allows for secure installation of child safety seats.
  • All-Wheel Disc Brakes: Provides better braking efficiency compared to drum brakes.

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS):

  • Level 2 ADAS: Includes features such as:
    • Adaptive Cruise Control: Maintains a set distance from the vehicle ahead.
    • Forward Collision Warning: Alerts the driver to potential collisions.
    • Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB): Automatically applies the brakes to prevent or mitigate collisions.
    • Lane Keep Assist: Helps keep the vehicle within its lane.
    • Traffic Sign Recognition: Detects and displays traffic signs.
    • High Beam Assist: Automatically adjusts the high beams based on oncoming traffic.

Crash Test Ratings:

  • Bharat NCAP: 5-star safety rating, indicating excellent occupant protection.

The XUV 3XO's comprehensive suite of safety features positions it as one of the safest vehicles in its segment, enhancing its appeal to safety-conscious buyers.

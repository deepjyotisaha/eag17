🎯 Executive Summary

This report provides a detailed overview of the Mahindra XUV 3XO, a compact SUV known for its powerful engine options, advanced safety features, and stylish design. The report covers key aspects such as engine specifications, safety technologies, interior and exterior design elements, and unique selling points that position the XUV 3XO competitively in the market.

⚙️ Engine Specifications

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers multiple engine options:

  • 1.2-litre Turbo Petrol (MPFI): Produces 110 Hp and 200 Nm of torque.
  • 1.2-litre Turbo Petrol (TGDi): Delivers 130 Hp and 230 Nm of torque. This engine is the most powerful in its class.
  • 1.5-litre Diesel: Generates 115 Hp and 300 Nm of torque.

The transmission options include manual, AMT, and a 6-speed torque converter. The mileage ranges from 17.96 kmpl to 21.2 kmpl depending on the engine and transmission type [1].

🛡️ Safety Features

The XUV 3XO is equipped with Level 2 ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), which includes features such as forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control. It also boasts a 5-star Bharat NCAP Safety Rating. Standard safety features include:

  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)
  • Driver Airbag
  • Passenger Airbag

🛋️ Interior Design

The interior of the XUV 3XO features soft-touch materials on the dashboard and door pads, enhancing the premium feel. Key interior features include:

  • Panoramic sunroof (largest in the segment)
  • Automatic climate control
  • Multi-function steering wheel
  • Ventilated seats

💻 Technology

The XUV 3XO is loaded with technology, including a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system and over 60 connected car features. Additional tech features include:

  • Real-time vehicle tracking
  • Cruise control
  • Parking sensors (front and rear)

🌟 Unique Selling Points

The Mahindra XUV 3XO stands out due to several unique selling points:

  • Powerful Engine: The 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine (TGDi) is the most powerful in its class, delivering 130PS/230Nm.
  • Panoramic Sunroof: The largest sunroof in the segment provides an open and airy feel to the cabin.
  • ADAS Level 2: Advanced safety features enhance driver assistance and safety.
  • Spacious Boot: Offers a boot space of 364 litres [1].
  • Connected Car Features: Over 60 connected car features provide enhanced convenience and control.

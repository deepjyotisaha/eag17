🎨 Design and Styling

The Mahindra XUV 3XO features a distinctive design with vertically oriented headlights, C-shaped DRLs, and a new grille. The front bumper has pronounced cuts and creases, giving it a concept car-like appearance. The 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels enhance its muscular profile.

  • Headlights: Vertically oriented with C-shaped DRLs
  • Grille: New gloss panel running the XUV's width
  • Wheels: 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels
  • Ground Clearance: 201mm

engine and performance

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers both petrol and diesel engine options:

  • Petrol Engine: 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine, producing 130PS/230Nm
  • Diesel Engine: 1.5-litre diesel engine

It is available with both Manual & Automatic transmission. Depending upon the variant and fuel type the XUV 3XO has a mileage of 17.96 to 20.6 kmpl.

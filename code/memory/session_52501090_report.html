Mahindra XUV 3XO: Comprehensive Report

Overview

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is Mahindra's latest entry into the competitive compact SUV segment in India, designed to appeal to a wide range of customers seeking a blend of style, performance, and advanced technology. The vehicle aims to outperform rivals such as the Tata Nexon, Hyundai Venue, and Maruti Suzuki Brezza by offering a compelling package of features and value.

Key Features

General Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO distinguishes itself with a bold and athletic exterior design. Key exterior highlights include:

  • Dimensions: The vehicle boasts ample ground clearance, typically around 201 mm, which enhances its suitability for varied terrains.
  • Lighting: The XUV 3XO features LED Daytime Running Lights (DRLs) and LED headlamps, providing enhanced visibility and a modern aesthetic. Infinity tail lamps add a distinctive touch to the rear design.
  • Wheels: Depending on the variant, the XUV 3XO comes with 16-inch steel wheels with covers or 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels, contributing to its sporty appearance.

Comfort and Convenience:

  • Command seating position for an elevated driving experience.
  • Skyroof (panoramic sunroof) available in higher variants, enhancing the cabin's ambiance.
  • Premium leatherette seats and soft-touch leatherette on door trims and dashboard for a luxurious feel.
  • Cooled glovebox with illumination for added convenience.
  • Passive keyless entry for easy access.
  • Front armrest with storage and console with cup holders.

Digital Features

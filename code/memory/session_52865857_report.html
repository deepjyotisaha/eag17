Mahindra XUV 3XO Overview

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a subcompact SUV that offers a blend of performance, modern features, and safety. It's designed to provide a premium driving experience within its segment, competing with vehicles like the Tata Nexon, Hyundai Venue, and Kia Sonet.

Engine Options: Petrol and Diesel

Price Range: Rs. 7.99 - 15.80 Lakh

Luxury Features Analysis

The Mahindra XUV 3XO incorporates several features that aim to elevate the driving and ownership experience, positioning it as a more luxurious option within the highly competitive subcompact SUV segment.

  • Panoramic Sunroof: Enhances the cabin's ambiance, providing an open and airy feel.
  • Premium Interiors: Soft-touch materials, leatherette seats, and a dual-tone cabin theme.
  • 10.25-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System: Android Auto and Apple CarPlay compatibility.
  • Harman Kardon Sound System: Premium audio experience.

These features collectively aim to deliver a more premium and satisfying driving and ownership experience.

Safety Features Analysis

The Mahindra XUV 3XO boasts a comprehensive suite of safety features, including active and passive safety systems, as well as driver-assistance technologies.

  • 5-Star Bharat NCAP Rating: High level of protection for both adult and child occupants.
  • 6 Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags.
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC): Maintains vehicle stability during emergency maneuvers.
  • Level 2 ADAS: Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (Adaptive Cruise Control, Lane Departure Warning, etc.).

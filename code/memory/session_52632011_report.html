Performance

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers both petrol and diesel engine options. A powerful 1.2-litre TGDi engine is mated with a 6-speed torque converter gearbox. It also boasts the most powerful engine at 130 bhp and diesel torque of 300 Nm in its segment. [1]

Safety

The XUV 3XO is equipped with safety features like Level 2 ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), 6 airbags, ESP (Electronic Stability Program), ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), all-wheel disc brakes, TPMS (Tyre Pressure Monitoring System), and seatbelt reminders for all occupants. [2] It is expected to score a 5-star rating, building upon the XUV300's previous safety credentials.

Interior

The interior of the XUV 3XO features a fully digital 10.25-inch instrument cluster and a panoramic sunroof which is a segment-first. [3] It offers steering modes (sport, normal, and comfort). The car also has dual-zone AC. [1] An electronic parking brake with auto hold is available in the top-spec AX7 L trim. [3]

Exterior

The XUV 3XO features a refreshed exterior design that may be polarizing. It has 17-inch alloy wheels which is unique in its segment. [1]

Technology

The XUV 3XO comes with Level 2 ADAS, a 360-degree camera, Harman Kardon audio system, and wireless smartphone connectivity. [2]

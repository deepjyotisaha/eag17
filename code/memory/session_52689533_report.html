Executive Summary

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compact SUV designed for modern urban drivers, blending bold design with advanced technology. Key features include versatile powertrain options, advanced safety features, premium interiors, and modern exterior design. Prices range from ₹7.99 lakh to ₹15.80 lakh, making it an attractive choice in the subcompact SUV segment [1].

Safety Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO prioritizes safety with features like Level 2 Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), which includes forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control. It also includes a 360-degree camera, six airbags, and electronic stability control [1].

  • Level 2 ADAS
  • 360-degree camera
  • Six airbags
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • All-wheel disc brakes
  • TPMS (Tyre Pressure Monitoring System)
  • Seatbelt reminders for all seats

Performance Features

The XUV 3XO offers both petrol and diesel engine options. The 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine produces 130PS/230Nm, making it one of the most powerful in its class [2]. It is available with both manual and automatic transmissions.

  • Engine Options: 1.2-litre turbo-petrol, 1.5-litre diesel
  • Max Power: 128.73bhp@5000rpm (petrol)
  • Max Torque: 230Nm@1500-3750rpm (petrol)
  • Transmission: Manual & Automatic
  • Mileage: 17.96 to 20.6 kmpl

Technology Features

Inside, the XUV 3XO features dual 10.25-inch displays for the infotainment system and driver's instrument cluster. It also includes wireless charging, a cooled glove box, and connected car technology [1].

  • Dual 10.25-inch displays
  • Wireless charging
  • Cooled glove box
  • Connected car technology
  • Harman Kardon audio system
  • Wireless smartphone connectivity
  • Auto headlamps & wipers

Comfort Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers a panoramic sunroof, soft-touch materials on the dashboard and door pads, and ample space for occupants. It also includes features like dual-zone climate control with rear AC vents [5].

  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Soft touch material on dashboard and door pads
  • Dual-zone climate control
  • Rear AC vents
  • Ventilated seats (in some variants)

Pricing and Variants

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is available in multiple variants with prices ranging from ₹7.99 lakh to ₹15.80 lakh (ex-showroom). It includes both petrol and diesel options, with manual and automatic transmissions [5].

Exterior Design

The XUV 3XO features a bold front grille, connected LED tail lamps, and a panoramic sunroof, enhancing its visual appeal. It has a refreshed exterior design that may be polarizing [4].

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers both petrol and diesel engine options to cater to different driving preferences. The 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine is the most powerful in its class, delivering 128.73bhp and 230Nm of torque. The diesel engine provides excellent torque for effortless highway drives.

  • 1.5-litre Diesel Engine: Provides strong performance and fuel efficiency.
  • Transmission: Available with both Manual & Automatic transmission options.

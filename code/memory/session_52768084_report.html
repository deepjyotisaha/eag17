🛡️ Safety Features & Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS) Overview

Mahindra has made safety a paramount consideration in the XUV 3XO's design, aiming for the highest safety standards and integrating a comprehensive suite of active and passive protective systems.

  • Comprehensive Airbag Protection: A significant safety highlight is the provision of 6 airbags as standard across all trims, starting from MX1. This commitment to democratizing safety ensures comprehensive protection for all occupants in the event of a collision, far exceeding segment norms.
  • Advanced Stability Control Systems: The vehicle is equipped with sophisticated Electronic Stability Control (ESC) and Hill Hold Control (HHC).   • Superior Braking Performance: The inclusion of All 4 Disc Brakes (Front & Rear) across all variants contributes to superior stopping power, reduced braking distances, and enhanced fade resistance compared to drum brake setups, ensuring greater safety in emergency braking situations.
  • Safety Rating Commitment: Mahindra has engineered the XUV 3XO to meet the highest B-NCAP safety rating, reflecting a strong proactive commitment to passenger safety and structural integrity. This commitment provides a high level of assurance to consumers regarding the vehicle's crashworthiness.
  • Occupant Restraint Systems: The vehicle includes essential safety features like seat belt reminders for all passengers and 3-point seat belts for all passengers, reinforcing the importance of proper restraint usage for maximum protection.
  • Level 2 Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS): A groundbreaking feature for this segment is the First-in-segment Level 2 ADAS (1R1V - 1 Radar, 1 Vision Camera). This advanced suite integrates functionalities such as:
    • Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC): Automatically adjusts vehicle speed to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead.
    • Lane Keeping Assist (LKA): Helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane through subtle steering inputs.
    • Forward Collision Warning (FCW) & Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB): Alerts the driver to potential frontal collisions and can autonomously apply brakes to mitigate or avoid an impact.
    • High Beam Assist (HBA): Automatically switches between high and low beams based on traffic and lighting conditions.
    • Traffic Sign Recognition (TSR): Displays detected traffic signs (e.g., speed limits) on the instrument cluster.
    This comprehensive ADAS suite significantly enhances active safety, reduces driver fatigue, and provides an unparalleled layer of protection in its class.
  • Enhanced Parking & Maneuvering Aids: For urban convenience and enhanced awareness, the XUV 3XO features a First-in-segment Electronic Parking Brake with Auto Hold, which simplifies stop-and-go traffic. Additionally, a First-in-segment 360-degree surround view system with blind view monitor provides a comprehensive view of the vehicle's surroundings, greatly assisting in parking, navigating tight spaces, and reducing blind spots, thereby preventing low-speed collisions.

    • Superior Ride Comfort: The system effectively absorbs shocks from potholes and rough roads, providing a remarkably smooth and compliant ride, even over challenging Indian road networks.
    • Enhanced Stability & Handling: By optimally managing body roll and pitch, MTV-CL ensures improved stability at higher speeds and more predictable handling during cornering, instilling greater driver confidence.
  • Customizable Steering Modes: The First-in-segment 3 Smart Steering Modes (Comfort, Normal, Sport) allow drivers to tailor the steering feel and responsiveness to their preference.

Overview

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compact SUV that aims to provide a blend of style, performance, and advanced features. It competes with other popular models in the Indian automotive market, offering a fresh design and comfortable interiors. Mahindra has positioned this vehicle as a must-buy in the compact SUV segment, emphasizing convenience and comfort across various terrains.

Key Highlights:

  • Design: Athletic stance with ample ground clearance (201 mm).
  • Lighting: LED DRLs and headlamps, infinity tail lamps.
  • Wheels: 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels.
  • Interior: Skyroof, soft-touch leatherette, premium leatherette seats, cooled glovebox, and passive keyless entry.

Engine and Performance

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers multiple engine options to cater to different driving preferences. These include:

  • 1.2L Petrol Engine: Produces 110 PS and 200 Nm of torque, suitable for city driving.
  • 1.5L CRDI Diesel Engine: Delivers 116 PS and 300 Nm of torque, ideal for highway driving.
  • 1.2-litre turbo-petrol engine: Makes 130PS/230Nm, noted as most powerful in its class.

The vehicle provides a smooth and comfortable ride with refined suspension and responsive steering. It has been tested extensively, including high-speed runs and slalom tests, to ensure it meets performance expectations.

Engine Specifications:

  • Engine Type: mStallion (TGDi) engine
  • Displacement: 1197 cc
  • Max Power: 128.73bhp@5000rpm
  • Max Torque: 230Nm@1500-3750rpm
  • Transmission: Available with Automatic & Manual transmission.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO features a range of safety technologies, including:

  • Airbags: Up to 6 airbags for comprehensive protection.
  • Braking System: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) and all four disc brakes.
  • Stability: Electronic Stability Control (ESC) to maintain vehicle stability.
  • Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS): Includes features like forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control in higher variants.

Technology and Connectivity

The XUV 3XO integrates smart connectivity features for an enhanced driving experience:

  • Infotainment System: High-definition touchscreen for infotainment and navigation.
  • Audio System: Harman Kardon 7-speaker audio system for premium sound quality.
  • Connectivity: ADRENOX Connect technology for remote control of car features.
  • Smart Features: Built-in Amazon Alexa, wireless charging (from MX3 Pro variant onwards), Apple CarPlay and Android Auto connectivity.

Interior and Exterior Design

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is designed to offer both comfort and style.

Interior:

  • Seating: Premium leatherette seats, adjustable headrests, and a front armrest with storage.
  • Comfort: Cooled glovebox with illumination, passive keyless entry, and rear AC vents.
  • Features: Skyroof, soft-touch leatherette on door trims and dashboard, dual-zone automatic climate control.

Exterior:

  • Design Elements: LED DRLs, infinity tail lamps, and 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels.
  • Practicality: Ample ground clearance of 201 mm for varied terrains.
  • Dimensions: Length of 3990 mm, width of 1821 mm, and a wheelbase of 2600 mm.

Additional Features and Variants

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is available in multiple variants, including MX series and AX series, catering to a range of customer preferences and budgets. Prices range from approximately Rs 7.99 lakh to Rs 15.79 lakh (ex-showroom India), with varied features across trims.

  • MX Series: Focus on essential features at a competitive price.
  • AX Series: Higher-end trims with advanced technology and premium comfort features like 360 cameras and ADAS.

Final Thoughts

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compelling option in the compact SUV market, offering a blend of style, performance, safety, and technology. Its multiple engine options, variant choices, and modern features make it a versatile choice for a wide range of consumers. With its competitive pricing and focus on comfort and convenience, the XUV 3XO stands out as a strong contender in its segment.

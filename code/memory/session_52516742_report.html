💰 Pricing and Trim Levels

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is priced from Rs 7.99 lakh to Rs 15.79 lakh (ex-showroom India). It is available in 12 trim levels, offering a wide range of options to cater to different customer preferences and budgets.

Engine and Performance

The XUV 3XO offers multiple engine options:

  • 1.2-litre turbo-petrol: 111hp
  • 1.2-litre TGDI petrol: 131hp
  • 1.5-litre diesel engine: 117hp

The 1.2L TGDI engine accelerates from 0 to 60 km/h in 4.5 seconds. A 6-speed manual gearbox is standard across the engine range. Petrol engines get a 6-speed torque converter automatic gearbox, while the diesel gets a 6-speed AMT unit.

Safety Features

Key safety features include:

  • 6 airbags
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC) with Hill Hold Control (HHC)
  • All 4 disc brakes

High-end trims come with Level 2 ADAS. The vehicle meets the highest B-NCAP safety rating, ensuring a secure driving experience.

Design Highlights

The design highlights of the XUV 3XO include:

  • Bold silhouette
  • Ivory-colored interiors with soft-touch leatherette
  • R17 alloy wheels
  • First-in-segment SkyroofTM (sunroof)

The SUV has a best-in-class wheelbase, occupant space, and legroom, with a cargo area of 364L.

Technology and Features

Key technology features include:

  • Twin HD Screens (26.03 cm infotainment and digital cluster)
  • Harman Kardon Premium Audio
  • 360-degree surround view system
  • Dual Zone Climate Control
  • 65 W USB-C charging
  • Electronic Parking brake with Auto Hold
  • 3 Smart Steering Modes

The XUV 3XO offers a best-in-class forward visibility of 23.7 degrees.

Competitive Positioning

The Mahindra XUV 3XO competes with other sub-compact SUVs in the Indian market, such as the Tata Nexon, Maruti Suzuki Brezza, Hyundai Venue, and Kia Sonet. The XUV 3XO aims to offer a superior value proposition by combining performance, safety, and technology at a competitive price point. Its unique features, such as the SkyroofTM and Level 2 ADAS, differentiate it from the competition. The aggressive pricing strategy further enhances its competitive edge, making it an attractive option for customers seeking a feature-rich and stylish SUV.

Features Report.

Executive Summary.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers a compelling combination of luxury and safety features across its variants. The higher trims include advanced safety technologies such as Level 2 ADAS, making it a strong contender in the subcompact SUV market. The XUV 3XO is available in multiple variants, offering a range of engine options including petrol and diesel. Key features include a panoramic sunroof, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system, and Level 2 ADAS. The vehicle aims to balance comfort, convenience, and safety to attract a broad customer base.

Luxury Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO incorporates a range of features designed to enhance comfort, convenience, and the overall driving experience. These features aim to provide a premium feel and cater to the evolving demands of modern car buyers.

  • Panoramic Sunroof: Available on higher variants like the AX5 L and AX7 L, providing an open and airy feel to the cabin. This feature enhances the aesthetic appeal and perceived value of the vehicle.
  • 10.25-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System: A key component of the car's entertainment and connectivity features, offering intuitive controls for audio, navigation, and other vehicle functions. Available from the MX2 variant onwards.
  • Wireless Android Auto and Apple CarPlay: Enables seamless smartphone integration, allowing users to access their favorite apps, music, and navigation directly on the car's infotainment screen. Wireless connectivity eliminates the need for cables, adding convenience (MX3 onwards).
  • Cruise Control: Standard on higher variants, helping reduce driver fatigue on long journeys by maintaining a constant speed. This contributes to a more relaxed and comfortable driving experience (MX3 onwards).
  • Premium Upholstery: The use of high-quality materials in the seats and interior trim enhances the overall sense of luxury and refinement.
  • Steering-mounted audio controls: Allows the driver to control the audio without taking their hands off the steering wheel, improving safety and convenience.
  • Wireless Phone Charger: Offers a convenient way to charge compatible smartphones, eliminating the need for charging cables. Available on higher variants.

Safety Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of features designed to protect occupants and mitigate potential risks. These features are available across different variants, showcasing Mahindra's commitment to safety.

  • 6 Airbags: Standard across all variants, providing crucial protection in the event of a collision. These include driver, passenger, side, and curtain airbags.
  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC): Helps prevent skidding and loss of control by automatically applying brakes to individual wheels when it detects a loss of traction. This is a critical safety feature, particularly in challenging driving conditions.
  • All-Wheel Disc Brakes: Offer superior stopping power compared to drum brakes, providing shorter stopping distances and enhanced safety. All-wheel disc brakes are standard across the XUV 3XO range.
  • ISOFIX Child Seat Anchors: Provide a secure and convenient way to install child seats, ensuring that children are properly restrained in the event of a collision.
  • Seatbelt Reminders for All Passengers: This feature alerts occupants if they are not wearing their seatbelts, encouraging them to buckle up and enhance their safety.
  • 3-Point Seatbelts for All Passengers: 3-point seatbelts provide superior restraint compared to lap belts, reducing the risk of injury in a crash.
  • Reverse Parking Sensors: Assist the driver during parking maneuvers by detecting obstacles behind the vehicle, reducing the risk of collisions.
  • Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS): Alerts the driver when the tire pressure is low, which can help prevent accidents and improve fuel economy.
  • Corner Braking Control: Improves vehicle stability during cornering by modulating brake pressure to individual wheels.
  • Hill Hold Assist: Prevents the vehicle from rolling backward on steep inclines, providing added safety and convenience.

Risks & Warnings.

Potential risks and warnings to consider when purchasing the Mahindra XUV 3XO:

  • Not all features are available across all variants, so it's crucial to check the variant-specific feature list before making a purchase decision. Certain features, like the panoramic sunroof and Level 2 ADAS, are limited to higher-end trims.
  • Level 2 ADAS features are not a substitute for attentive driving. Drivers should always remain vigilant and maintain control of the vehicle. Over-reliance on ADAS can lead to accidents.
  • The effectiveness of safety features can be affected by factors such as road conditions, weather, and driver behavior. These features provide assistance but do not guarantee complete safety.
  • Real-world fuel efficiency may vary from the advertised figures, depending on driving habits and conditions.
  • Ensure that the vehicle meets your specific needs in terms of space and practicality, as the XUV 3XO is a subcompact SUV and may not be suitable for larger families or those requiring ample cargo space.

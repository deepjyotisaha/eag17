Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO has 1 Diesel Engine and 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 1498 cc while the Petrol engine is 1197 cc . It is available with Manual & Automatic transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the XUV 3XO has a mileage of 17.96 to 20.6 kmpl. The XUV 3XO is a 5 seater 3 cylinder car and has length of 3990 mm, width of 1821 mm and a wheelbase of 2600.

Safety

The Mahindra XUV 3XO has secured a 5-star safety rating from Bharat NCAP. The SUV was crash-tested by Bharat NCAP in October 2024. The XUV 3XO scored 29.36 points out of 32 in adult occupant protection and 43 points out of 49 in child occupant protection.

  • 5-star rating for adult occupant protection.
  • Child occupant protection receives 5-star rating.
  • Standard safety features include 6 airbags, ESC, reverse parking sensors, & more.
  • Level 2 ADAS available.

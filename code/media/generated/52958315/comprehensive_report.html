Mahindra XUV 3XO Overview.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is a compact SUV that blends bold design with advanced technology. Launched in April 2024, it is a refreshed version of the XUV300, featuring significant upgrades in design and technology. The SUV boasts a bold front grille, connected LED tail lamps, and a panoramic sunroof. Inside, it offers dual 10.25-inch displays for the infotainment system and driver's instrument cluster, wireless charging, and a cooled glove box. It comes with level 2 ADAS and 6 airbags and electronic stability control.

This section provides a general overview of the Mahindra XUV 3XO, including its design, features, and key specifications.

  • Safety is prioritized with Level 2 ADAS and six airbags.

Detailed Analysis of Luxury and Safety Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO offers a range of luxury and safety features that enhance the driving experience and ensure passenger safety. Luxury features include a panoramic sunroof, premium audio system, wireless charging, and a cooled glove box. Safety features include six airbags, Electronic Stability Control (ESC), ABS, all-wheel disc brakes, TPMS, seatbelt reminders for all passengers, and Level 2 ADAS. The ADAS features include forward collision warning, autonomous emergency braking, adaptive cruise control, and lane-keep assist.

Sales Performance Analysis.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO has demonstrated strong sales performance since its launch. In June 2025, the total sales figure of XUV 3XO was 7,089 units, which represents a 10.85% MoM decline. The SUV recorded 98,091 unit sales in FY2024-25 (April 2024 to March 2025 period) as against 53,962 units during the same period the previous fiscal, witnessing a YoY sales surge of 82%. The launch month of May 2024 saw 10,000 units dispatched, setting a strong tone right from the start. These figures demonstrate the growing demand for the XUV 3XO in the Indian market.

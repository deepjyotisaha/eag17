Japan Travel Handbook

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Note: This itinerary is a general framework due to limited specific data. Please customize based on your interests and budget.

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo: Airport transfer, check-in, explore Shinjuku (general activities).

Day 2: Tokyo: Visit historical sites like the Imperial Palace East Garden, explore cultural areas like Asakusa (Senso-ji Temple).

Day 3: Kyoto: Travel via Shinkansen, visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, explore Gion district.

Day 4: Kyoto: Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove (possible proposal location).

Day 5: Nara: Day trip from Kyoto, visit Todai-ji Temple, Nara Park (deer feeding).

Day 6: Hakone: Travel from Kyoto, enjoy views of Mount Fuji, Lake Ashi cruise (possible proposal location).

Day 7: Departure: Travel to airport, departure from Japan.

Safety Features

Safety is a top priority in the Mahindra XUV 3XO, with a comprehensive suite of features designed to protect occupants. The car has achieved a 5-star safety rating in Bharat NCAP crash tests. It includes six airbags, Electronic Stability Control (ESC), Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), and ISOFIX child seat mounts. The XUV 3XO also features Level 2 Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), including adaptive cruise control, lane-keep assist, and auto emergency braking (AEB). These safety features aim to provide a secure and confident driving experience.

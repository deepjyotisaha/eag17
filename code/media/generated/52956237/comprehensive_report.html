Mahindra XUV 3XO Features.

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is designed to offer a blend of performance, comfort, and technology. It comes with multiple engine options, including a 1.2L turbo petrol and a 1.5L diesel engine. The XUV 3XO offers a spacious interior, a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system, and a panoramic sunroof. Key features include Level 2 ADAS, multiple airbags, and connected car technology. The XUV 3XO has a length of 3990 mm, a width of 1821 mm, and a wheelbase of 2600 mm. Its design includes LED DRLs, infinity tail lamps, and 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels.

Safety Features.

Safety is a key priority for the Mahindra XUV 3XO, which has achieved a 5-star safety rating in Bharat NCAP crash tests. It includes active and passive safety systems, such as multiple airbags, ABS with EBD, and electronic stability control. The XUV 3XO also features Level 2 ADAS with adaptive cruise control, lane-keep assist, and auto emergency braking. The robust build quality and advanced driver assistance systems ensure passenger protection in various driving conditions. The vehicle is equipped with ISOFIX child seat anchorages for secure child seating.

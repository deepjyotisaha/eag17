Mahindra XUV 3XO Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO is designed to offer a blend of style, comfort, and performance. Key features include a striking exterior with LED DRLs and headlamps, a comfortable interior with premium leatherette seats, and smart connectivity with a Harman Kardon 7-speaker audio system. The XUV 3XO also boasts a high-definition touchscreen system for infotainment and navigation, along with built-in Amazon Alexa for entertainment. It also includes Level 2 ADAS. The car also features 17-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels for that sporty look.

This section details the key features of the Mahindra XUV 3XO, highlighting its design, interior, connectivity, and performance aspects.

  • Striking exterior design with LED DRLs and headlamps.
  • Comfortable interior with premium leatherette seats and Skyroof.
  • Smart connectivity with Harman Kardon audio system and touchscreen infotainment.
  • 1.2L mStallion TCMPFi engine delivering a power of 82 kW and torque of 200 Nm.

Luxury and Safety Features

The Mahindra XUV 3XO prioritizes safety with features such as six airbags, ESC with Hill Hold Control (HHC), and All 4 Disc Brakes. The top-end variant, AX7 Luxury, features a panoramic sunroof, advanced infotainment options, and a full suite of safety features including Level 2 ADAS. Level 2 ADAS includes forward collision warning, adaptive cruise control, traffic sign recognition and more. The vehicle has also received a five-star safety rating in the Bharat NCAP crash tests, making it one of the safest options in its category.

This section provides a detailed analysis of the luxury and safety features of the Mahindra XUV 3XO, highlighting its technologies, materials, and safety ratings.

  • Six airbags, ESC with Hill Hold Control (HHC), and All 4 Disc Brakes for enhanced safety.
  • Level 2 ADAS with features like adaptive cruise control and traffic sign recognition.
  • Five-star safety rating in the Bharat NCAP crash tests.
  • Panoramic sunroof and advanced infotainment options in the AX7 Luxury variant.
